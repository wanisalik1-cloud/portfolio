02 — The Journey
Ongoing Thread

The Ground.

My visual practice begins with the landscape of Kashmir. Moving away from heavily romanticized depictions of the valley, I work directly with the terrain through en plein air painting. These works run constantly alongside my other projects—they are foundational studies of light, season, and geography, documenting what is actually here.

Selected Field Works
Est. 2020

The Collective.

Working independently highlighted a stark reality: the spaces required to make, critique, and encounter art in this region are often absent. In 2020, I co-founded Alfaaz Collective. It is an infrastructure project running parallel to my art. Through sustained club programming covering philosophy, photography, and literature, it creates a breathing space for Kashmiri creative voices.
