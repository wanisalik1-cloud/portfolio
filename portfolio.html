01 — Practice

A practice
rooted in place.

My work moves between landscape and language — the valleys of Kashmir as both subject and site of inquiry. Through en plein air painting, I work directly with light, season, and terrain, making images that resist the scenic and the nostalgic. Alongside this, I organize through Alfaaz Collective: building exhibitions, workshops, and clubs that create the conditions for others to make and encounter art in a region where those conditions are often absent. These two practices — making and organizing — are not separate. Both are concerned with attention: to what is here, and to what could be.

03 — Alfaaz Collective

Building
the conditions.

alfaazcollective.vercel.app ↗

Alfaaz Collective is an art, culture, and literature organization co-founded in Kashmir in 2020. Through exhibitions, en plein air sessions, workshops, and sustained club programming — covering photography, philosophy, art & craft, literature, and film — it creates ongoing space for Kashmiri creative voices, particularly at the intersection of art, language, and cultural memory.
